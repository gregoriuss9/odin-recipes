Rendang Daging

Description

Rendang daging is a popular Indonesian beef dish renowned for its bold and aromatic flavors. Prepared by slow-cooking beef in a harmonious blend of coconut milk and a medley of spices, including galangal, lemongrass, turmeric, garlic, and chili peppers, rendang daging offers a rich and indulgent culinary experience. The extended cooking process results in tender beef coated in a thick, flavorful sauce, creating a perfect balance of savory, sweet, and spicy notes. Often served during special occasions, rendang daging is celebrated for its cultural significance and has gained widespread appreciation for its delicious and complex taste.

Ingredients

  • Beef (usually in chunks or slices)
  • Coconut milk
  • Galangal (or ginger), grated or sliced
  • Lemongrass, bruised and sliced
  • Turmeric, fresh or ground
  • Garlic, minced
  • Shallots, finely chopped
  • Red chili peppers, finely chopped (adjust quantity based on spiciness preference)
  • Kaffir lime leaves
  • Tamarind paste or tamarind juice
  • Bay leaves
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cloves
  • Cardamom pods
  • Salt and sugar (to taste)

Steps

  1. Prepare the beef by cutting it into chunks or slices.
  2. In a pot, combine coconut milk, grated or sliced galangal, bruised and sliced lemongrass, turmeric (fresh or ground), minced garlic, and finely chopped shallots.
  3. Add finely chopped red chili peppers (adjust quantity based on spiciness preference), kaffir lime leaves, tamarind paste or tamarind juice, bay leaves, a cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom pods.
  4. Place the pot on the stove over medium heat and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Add the prepared beef to the simmering mixture and stir well.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and let the beef cook slowly, allowing the coconut milk to thicken and coat the beef. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  7. Continue cooking until the beef is tender and the sauce has thickened to your liking.
  8. Season with salt and sugar to taste.
  9. Once done, remove from heat and serve the rendang daging while it's still warm.
