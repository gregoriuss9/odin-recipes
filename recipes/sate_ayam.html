Sate Ayam

Description

Sate ayam, or chicken satay, is a popular Indonesian dish known for its delightful skewered and grilled chicken. The chicken pieces are marinated in a flavorful mixture of soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and various spices, imparting a savory and aromatic taste. Skewered onto sticks, the marinated chicken is then grilled or charbroiled to perfection. Sate ayam is often served with a side of peanut sauce, adding a rich and nutty complement to the tender and succulent chicken. This dish is a beloved street food and a favorite at social gatherings, showcasing the vibrant flavors of Indonesian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (various, depending on preference)
  • Skewers
  • Peanut sauce (for serving)

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken by cutting it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix the chicken with soy sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, and a blend of spices according to your preference. Ensure the chicken is well-coated and marinate for at least an hour or longer for enhanced flavor.
  3. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers, evenly distributing them.
  4. Grill or charbroil the skewered chicken until fully cooked and slightly charred on the edges.
  5. While grilling, baste the chicken with any remaining marinade to keep it moist and flavorful.
  6. Once cooked, remove the sate ayam from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
  7. Serve the sate ayam hot with a side of peanut sauce for dipping.
